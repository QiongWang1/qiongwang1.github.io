Qiong Wang

I am a Master's graduate in Computer Science from Boston University, passionate about applying artificial intelligence to biomedical imaging. My research interests include deep learning for healthcare, bio image processing, medical image analysis and neuroscience. I am currently working as a Research Assistant at Prof. Donglai Wei's lab, focusing on AI for Neuroscience.
